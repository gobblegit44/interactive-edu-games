The Pattern Game

AI learns by looking at lots and lots of words and finding patterns.

Let's play a game! Can you guess what word comes next?

"Pizza" is a good guess! AI models learn that after "I love to eat", foods like "pizza" often come next.

This is called "predicting the next word" - the AI has seen lots of sentences and knows what words usually come after other words.

Learning from Patterns

AI models learn from millions of books, stories, and websites to understand patterns.
